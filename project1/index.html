US Census Data Map

I used the data from the US census to create an interactive map that displays the information provided by the census data. You can cycle through different data sets at the bottom of the page including information on the gender distrubition of the people living in that state, the different ethnicities, the percentage of people with and without healthcare, and the amount of people in poverty. The information shows up as pi charts representing each state.
